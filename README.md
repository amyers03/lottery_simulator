# lottery_simulator
A lottery simulator I built to help visualize pick odds under the new NBA lottery system being implemented in 2027.

Each simulation is built in increments to accomodate for each rule that affects the order. The first pick is drawn first, followed by picks 2 though 9, then followed by 10 through 12, then 13 through 16.

# takeaways
- Odds are flattened almost entirely across the board.
- There is now more of an advantage to be middling instead of worst in the league.
- The value of swaps have completely changed. If you are swapping between two teams who finished between the 4th and 10th worst records in the league, the team receiving the higher end of the swap has doubled their odds at the first pick.
- Top 4 pick protections have less value for the team trading them away, as even with the best odds you still have an over 60% chance of losing it.
- Relegation floor means teams with the three worst records have significantly higher chance of picking at 11 or 12 than at any other specific spot.
